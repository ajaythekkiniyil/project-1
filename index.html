The Amrita School of Ayurveda pursues a unique path in blending
ancient philosophies and science of health by nurturing a better breed
of Ayurvedic physicians, professionally world class and mentally
equipped to meet the challenges of the modern world. Amrita School
of Ayurveda, Amritapuri was established in the year 2004 and
managed by Mata Amritanandamayi Math Trust. The college has
developed into a prestigious centre for education in Ayurveda,
nationally as well as internationally, under the gracious guidance of its
founder and spiritual guru, Mata Amritanandamayi Devi
